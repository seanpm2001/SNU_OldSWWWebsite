Waterfall Bounce Time

Available upon Request

During This Time, You can bounce in the center of a 6 foot tall water fall. The water is 60-72 Degrees in the summer, 73-85 Degrees in the fall, 86-95 Degrees in the winter, And 59-74 Degrees In the spring

You can also adjust the water temperature ONLY in summer, fall, and spring
