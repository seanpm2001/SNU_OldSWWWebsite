<H1>Help Topic 1: Multiple Users</H1>

<H2>If you have a family sharing this game, you can create a Multiple users server to play multiple accounts at once. The second account is Free, but after that, each new account costs $0.99 to prevent account spam.</H2>
<H2>When playing multiple accounts, you can switch users, but if you try to switch to an active user, you will get a prompt, logging you out of the server</H2>
<H2>Server settings can be advanced, like you can set passwords to accounts, add pins, add questions and hints, just to make servers safer</H2>
<H2>You can also customize your accounts by adding custom profile pictures, backgrounds, adding themes, and much more (NOTE: customization features only unlock with servers with 3 people or more</H2>
<H2>You can set the server to run off a mobile hotspot (only recommended for short periods and high end phones) wifi boxes (recommended for constant server play) or LAN (recommended for small and multiple servers)</H2>
<H2>Everyone in your server will automatically be friended to you, only 1 person can go on an account at once though</H2>
<H2>The recommended server playime for mobile hotspot is below</H2>
<H3>2 People: up to 3 hours = 1 GB to 50 GB (Depending on quality and server usage)</H3>
<H3>3 People: up to 2 hours 45 minutes = 1.7 GB to 99 GB (Depending on quality and server usage)</H3>
<H3>4 People: up to 2 hours 30 minutes = 2.35 GB to 133 GB (Depending on quality and server usage)</H3>
<H3>5 People: up to 2 hours 15 minutes = 2.95 GB to 165 GB (Depending on quality and server usage)</H3>
<H3>6 People: up to 2 hours = 4 GB to 255 GB (Depending on quality and server usage)</H3>
<H3>7 People: up to 1 hour 30 minutes = 6 GB to 500 GB (Depending on quality and server usage)</H3>
<H2>The max player count for a mobile hotspot server is 7, to prevent system and server failure</H2>
<H2>Dial-Up is NOT Supported from Windows 98 and up, Mac OS 9 and up</H2>
